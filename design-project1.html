RebrandX: Corporate Identity Overhaul

Crafting a modern visual identity for a legacy tech company.

Project Overview

RebrandX approached us with the challenge of revitalizing their established brand to connect with a younger, more dynamic market. Their existing visual identity felt outdated and no longer reflected their innovative spirit. My role was to lead the design process from concept to final asset delivery.

The process involved an initial discovery phase, including competitor analysis and stakeholder interviews. This was followed by mood boarding, iterative logo design, and development of a comprehensive brand guide covering typography, color palettes, imagery style, and application mockups.

Design Goals

  • Modernize the brand perception.
  • Increase appeal to a millennial audience.
  • Ensure versatility across digital and print media.
  • Create a memorable and unique logo mark.

Tools & Software

Adobe Illustrator Adobe Photoshop Figma (for mockups) Google Slides (for presentation)
